BANGKOK

Bangkok, the capital of Thailand, is a city full of culture, street food, temples, cafes, rooftop bars, and creative local areas. It is where traditional Thai life meets a modern city lifestyle.

กรุงเทพมหานคร

Bangkok’s Thai name is กรุงเทพมหานคร or Krung Thep Maha Nakhon. The city is popular for travellers because it offers many different experiences in one place, from temples and local markets to modern shopping malls and nightlife.
